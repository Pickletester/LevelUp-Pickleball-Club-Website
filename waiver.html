Waiver & Release

Assumption of Risk

You acknowledge that there are inherent risks involved with any fitness, sports, and physical activity, including the use of courts and equipment, and participation in activities such as pickleball, badminton, and table tennis. Such risks may include serious bodily injury or death. You knowingly and voluntarily assume all risks of liability, loss, illness, death, or injury caused or arising out of the use of LevelUp Pickleball Club courts, equipment, services, facilities, or your participation in any activities at LevelUp Pickleball Club.

Health Attestation

You attest that you are fully able to participate in pickleball, badminton, table tennis, and other physical activities at the facility without undue risk, and you are not aware of any medical condition that would prevent safe participation.

Rules & Conduct

You shall abide by all safety guidelines, rules, instructions, signage, and regulations set by LevelUp Pickleball Club. You and your guests shall abide by LevelUp Pickleball Club's dress code at all times. Consumption of illegal drugs, tobacco products, or similar addictive substances is strictly prohibited. Violence of any kind is not permitted and will result in removal from the facility.

Release of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, you release, waive, and discharge LevelUp Pickleball Club, LLC, its owners, officers, employees, agents, and affiliates ("Released Parties") from any and all claims, demands, damages, or liability arising out of your participation in any activities at LevelUp Pickleball Club, including claims arising from negligence of the Released Parties.

Audio & Video Recording

LevelUp Pickleball Club's facilities — including all courts, common areas, lobbies, and surrounding premises — are equipped with audio and video recording systems that operate continuously. By entering the facility, you expressly consent to being recorded at all times while on the premises. This consent is given in accordance with the Pennsylvania Wiretap Act (18 Pa. C.S. § 5703) and all applicable federal and state laws.

Media Release

You grant LevelUp Pickleball Club, LLC a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, edit, publish, and distribute any audio, video, photographs, or other media captured of you while on premises for any lawful purpose, including marketing, social media, highlight reels, and instructional content. If you do not wish to grant this license, notify us at membership@LevelUpPickleballClub.com before your visit.

Arbitration

Any disputes arising out of or related to this agreement shall be resolved through binding individual arbitration. Both parties waive the right to a jury trial and to class actions or representative proceedings.

Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania.
