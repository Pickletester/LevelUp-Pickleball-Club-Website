Our Story

BUILT BY PLAYERS,
FOR PLAYERS

Wayne and Lisa Dollard — husband and wife and former collegiate tennis players — discovered pickleball in 2015 before it was really a "thing." They went on to found Pickleball Magazine, now in its 10th year, before turning their attention to building the finest indoor pickleball facility in the country right here in the south suburbs of Pittsburgh.

"We incorporated ideas from the best clubs around the country and added our own wish list of amenities." The result is LevelUp — 16 courts with Category 1 broadcast-quality lighting, a 7-layer US Open cushioned surface found at fewer than 1% of courts nationwide, a full bar and restaurant, speakeasy, golf simulator, pro shop, and ballroom.

Over the years the Dollards built LevelUp Pickleball Camps into a national platform now operating at 280 locations per year across 43 states — developing champions and helping everyday players level up their game.

Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED
QUESTIONS

Where is LevelUp Pickleball Club?
▼
LevelUp Pickleball Club is located at 33 Mayview Road, Canonsburg, PA 15317 — in the South Hills of Pittsburgh.
What are your hours?
▼
Sunday – Thursday: 7:00 a.m. – 10:00 p.m.
Friday – Saturday: 7:00 a.m. – 11:00 p.m.
How do I become a member?
▼
Visit our Member Portal at app.courtreserve.com or click "Member Portal" in the navigation. You can sign up online in minutes and start enjoying all member benefits immediately. You can also download our app "LevelUp Pickleball Club" for easy access anywhere.
How do I book a court?
▼
Courts can be booked through our Member Portal at app.courtreserve.com. Members get advance booking windows depending on their tier — Diamond gets 2 weeks advance, Gold gets 1 week, and Silver gets 2 days.
Can I rent courts?
▼
Yes! Members can reserve courts starting at $25 per hour, increasing during prime times (evenings & weekends). Non-members may also rent courts. See our Membership page for the full court rental fee schedule.
Do I have to have a membership to play?
▼
No — non-members are welcome! Guest drop-in fee is $20 per session (visitor pass included). However, if you play once a week or more, a membership will quickly save you money and give you many additional benefits.
Do you offer lessons?
▼
Absolutely! LevelUp is the #1 recognized name in pickleball instruction. We offer private lessons, team/couple lessons, Three and a Pro sessions, clinics, and multi-day camps. Our pros have taught over 20,000 students across 40+ states. Members receive 10% off all instruction.
What if I am a beginner?
▼
Welcome! Pickleball is one of the fastest-growing sports precisely because it's so easy to pick up. We offer beginner drop-in sessions (2.0–2.9), beginner-level clinics, and private lessons for players of all starting skill levels. Our pros will have you competing and having fun in no time.
What if I don't have anyone to play with?
▼
That's what drop-in play is for! Show up at any scheduled drop-in session and you'll be automatically paired with players of similar skill level. It's a great way to meet new people and make friends in the pickleball community.
Do you offer leagues?
▼
Yes! We offer Ladder Leagues (run several days per week for singles, doubles, and mixed doubles) and Team Leagues (spring, summer, and fall 6-person men's and women's leagues). Both are coming soon — watch for announcements in the Member Portal.
